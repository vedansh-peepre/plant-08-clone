What stage is your in?

🌱

Seed

Just starting - your plant is a tiny seed waiting to sprout

🌿

Seedling

Growing - your plant has sprouted and is developing its first leaves

🌳

Plant/Tree

Mature - your plant is fully grown and ready for care instructions

Not sure which stage your plant is in?

  • Seed: Plant hasn't sprouted yet or just sprouted
  • Seedling: Has leaves but still small and delicate
  • Plant/Tree: Mature size and actively growing or producing
