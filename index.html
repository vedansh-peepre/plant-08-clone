🌍 Master Your Soil

Loamy soil is BEST - 40% sand, 40% silt, 20% clay

Ideal: 6.0-7.0 pH
  • 💡 Add 3" compost yearly
  • 🧪 Test pH every spring
  • 🌿 Mulch for moisture

Spring
2-3x/week
Summer
3-4x/week
Fall
1-2x/week

1x/week
